Macaroni and Cheese

Description

This is a simple macaroni and cheese recipe that is both cheap, delicious, and easy-to-make.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounce box elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil.
  2. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stir occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes.
  3. Drain.
  4. Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  5. Stir the butter in flour, salt, and pepper until it is smooth, about 5 minutes.
  6. Slowly pour milk into butter-flour mixture while continously stirring until mixture is smooth and bubbling, about 5 minutes.
  7. Add cheddar cheese to milk mixture and stir until cheese is melted, 2-4 minutes.
  8. Fold macaroni into cheese sauce until coated.
